Crystal Collector

Instructions
You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you untill you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
